About us

Khadian Ventures Limited is a registered business under the registration of Business Names Act Cap 499, sec on 14 trading within the Republic of Kenya. It is a building works, roads works and supplies venture. It was registered in the year 2020 and since then has a breath of experience encompassing all sectors of the industry from supplies and distribu on, educa onal and leisure facilies,healthcare, industrial and commercial buildings, building refurbishment, heritage works and distribution facilities to large scale residential developments and small engineering projects such as drainage schemes and main water networks.

Contracts have been successfully undertaken all over Kenya with main area of works concentration being County, Nairobi and its environs which is easily serviced from our main oﬃce in Kitengela. Over the years the skills and management base of the company has grown and developed and our team of staﬀ and managers have an enthusiasm, competence, and commitment unequalled in the industry.

Competitive tendering, professional management, quality construction, an excellent safety record, and proven ability to meet expectations, and our cooperative and proactive approach, have ensured the satisfaction of Design Teams and clients in both the public and private sectors.

Mission

At Khadian Ventures Limited, our mission is to deliver excellence in building and road construction projects while providing top-quality supplies to our clients. We are committed to exceeding expectations by delivering innovative solutions, fostering sustainable practices, and building lasting relationships with our stakeholders.

Vision

Our vision at Khadian Ventures Limited is to be a leading provider of construction services and supplies, recognized for our unwavering commitment to quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. We aspire to drive positive change in the industry through innovation, professionalism, and ethical business practices

Core Values

Our core values are what make us succeed and deliver excellent works and services. We mainly concentrate on Quality, Integrity, Excellence, Safety, Collaboration, Sustainability, and Customer Focus.
